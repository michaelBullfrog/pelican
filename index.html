Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time of year for party boat fishing in Destin, FL?

The best time for party boat fishing in Destin is spring through fall, when the Gulf waters are warm and fish are most active. However, Pelican Adventures offers productive fishing trips year-round.

What fish can you catch on a private charter in Destin, FL?

Private charters in Destin commonly target red snapper, grouper, amberjack, king mackerel, mahi-mahi, tuna, and seasonal billfish like sailfish or swordfish.

How does swordfishing work in Destin, FL?

Destin swordfishing is done offshore using deep-drop techniques at depths of 1,000–1,800 feet. Trips are typically full-day charters due to the distance offshore.

Is parasailing in Destin, FL safe for beginners?

Yes. Parasailing in Destin is beginner-friendly. All operators follow strict safety standards, and no prior experience is required.

What should I bring for snorkeling in Destin, FL?

Guests should bring swimwear, sunscreen, towels, and water. Snorkeling gear is usually provided by the tour company.
